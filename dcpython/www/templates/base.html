Alex Clark

Executive Director, President and Founder of ZPUGDC, Inc./DC Python, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization serving the MD, VA and DC Python community since 2008. Alex was the Lead Organizer of the Plone Conference 2008 in Washington, D.C., sponsored by ZPUGDC, Inc. In addition, Alex has been organizing and running the (formerly) ZPUGDC (now) DC Python user group for over 15 years. He is originally from Baltimore, Maryland and has a B.S. in Computer Science from Loyola University. In 2004, Alex founded and is currently running his own open-source Python software development and consulting firm, ACLARK.NET, LLC

About

We promote and support the research, development and use of Python software in DC, Maryland and Virginia. We support the education of all Python users by organizing and hosting frequent instructional events to foster and facilitate academic research, development and learning as well as professional growth and networking.
